FAQ
Common questions
Is ZangFu HIPAA compliant?
ZangFu is not currently a HIPAA-certified platform. Do not enter patient names or other identifying information. Use initials or patient IDs only. We are actively working toward compliance for a future version.
How accurate are the clinical outputs?
ZangFu generates clinically informed drafts based on the findings you enter. All outputs should be reviewed and modified by the treating practitioner before finalizing in the medical record. It is a clinical tool, not a replacement for clinical judgment.
Does it integrate with my EHR?
Not yet. ZangFu currently generates notes you can copy and paste into any EHR system. Direct EHR integration is on the roadmap for a future release.
What styles of TCM does it support?
ZangFu is grounded in TCM pattern differentiation as taught in accredited programs across North America. It supports tongue and pulse-based diagnosis, zang-fu pattern theory, channel theory, herbal medicine, and integrative assessment including orthopedic and hara findings.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel anytime from your billing portal and you will not be charged again. Your access continues through the end of the billing period.
What is the Founding Member rate?
The first 20 subscribers lock in $19/month for life — even as the price increases for new users. This is our way of rewarding early adopters who help shape the product. Once 20 spots are filled, this rate is gone permanently.
